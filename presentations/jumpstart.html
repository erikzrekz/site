Lessons Learned 3Ô∏è‚É£

Seek out information systems

When you're remote,
knowledge and information can become scarce,
sort of like a form of currency that can be exchanged.

Discover and learn

  • Existing systems
  • Histories & Stories
  • Taxonimies & Hierarchies
  • Precedents
  • Shared mental models

Use Wikipedia as a reference, guide, and tool.

(Strongly recommend downloading their app and using the "save" feature for things that interest you.)

Know where to ask internally.

Understand the difference between async and sync.

Create the most valuable meeting notes possible.
