OS Zendesk

2015 - 2017. As a Tools Engineer for Circle, I spent a lot of time and effort creating deep integrations between our backoffice tools and Zendesk. In order to keep pace with our integrations requirements, I contributed to a popular node.js Zendesk client library often enough that I became a primary maintainer. In doing so I became exceptionally familiar with their APIs and featureset. Before Zendesk added their own dashboarding tool for agents, I built our team a custom one and open-sourced around the same time.
